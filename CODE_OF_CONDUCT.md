# Community Code of Conduct

Atmosphere Account welcomes contributors and users of every background,
identity, experience level, and perspective. We want the project to be a place
where people can disagree constructively and still feel safe participating.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and patient.
- Critique ideas and code without attacking people.
- Assume good intent while remaining open to correction.
- Respect privacy, consent, and the boundaries of accounts you do not own.
- Make room for newcomers and explain project-specific context.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, doxxing, deliberate
intimidation, sustained disruption, and publishing another person's private
information are not acceptable in project spaces or when representing the
project.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `contact@atmosphere.money`. Include links
or screenshots and any context needed to understand the incident. Do not use a
public issue for a sensitive report.

Maintainers may edit or remove content, issue a warning, temporarily restrict
participation, or permanently ban a participant. Reports will be handled as
confidentially as practical, with attention to the safety of the reporter and
affected community members.

This policy applies to GitHub, Tangled, project-run community spaces, and public
events where someone is representing Atmosphere Account.
